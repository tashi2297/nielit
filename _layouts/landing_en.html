About the Project

Sikkim is one of the most beautiful State located in the north-eastern part of India. Protected by the might of the majestic Kanchenzonga and blessed by the chanting of the holy gods, Sikkim magnificently rests on the lap of the nature amidst mystical tranquillity.

  • With 5.5L domestic visitors in 2011.
  • Around 14.2L visitors in 2019
  • .23L foreign visitors in 2011 to 1.33L visitor in 2019

The State Government and the Department of Tourism & Civil Aviation since time has been instrumental in promoting the state as a prominent tourist avenue with the prime motive of allowing the enthusiastic explorer to witness the marvellous natural beauty and simulatenously uplifting the economic prosperity of the stakeholders, a win-win situation for all.
